Frequently Asked Questions

What is Dopi Note?

Dopi Note is an AI meeting assistant app that generates transcripts and summaries in just 1 minute. It features exclusive terminology correction and highly flexible summary formats, available on both iOS and Android.

Are my recordings and notes secure?

Absolutely. Your privacy is our top priority: • Audio stays on device → Original recordings are always stored only on your phone • Data can stay offline → Switch to local mode and all data stays on your phone • Cloud is encrypted → After login, text data is encrypted and backed up for cross-device access

Is it free? How does billing work?

• Free to start → New users get 3 hours free upon registration • No subscription pressure → No monthly or annual fees; pay-per-hour plans, buy as needed, credits never expire

Why should I log in?

Logging in makes things more convenient: • Cross-device sync → Seamlessly access all your notes on phone and tablet • Auto backup → Safely back up your work, no worries when switching phones

What languages are supported? Does it translate?

• Auto language detection → You don't need to do anything, the app automatically detects multiple languages • Auto summary translation → Summaries are automatically translated to your preferred language, so you instantly understand foreign-language meetings!

Is there a recording time limit?

No limit! However, we recommend splitting long meetings into segments for the best results and easier management.

Can I customize the summary format?

AI automatically selects the best format for your content. You can also choose from concise, detailed, bullet points, action items, or custom formats.

How do I import external audio files?

Super easy, two ways: • Share from any app → Tap "Share" in LINE, cloud drives, etc. • Import from within the app → Tap the "+" button and select files from your phone (supports mp3, m4a, wav, and more)

How do I export meeting notes?

One-tap share! Save as Word, PDF, TXT plain text, or directly copy the content to send to others.

How is Dopi Note different from other recording apps?

Dopi Note stands out with its highly flexible summary formats, local-mode privacy protection, and flexible pay-per-hour billing — no subscription required.

How accurate is the transcription?

AI recognition accuracy depends on recording quality. In quiet environments with clear audio, accuracy is very high. We recommend keeping your phone close to the speaker and avoiding excessive background noise for the best results.

Will my data be used to train AI?

No. Your recordings and notes are for your personal use only. We do not use any user data for AI model training. Your privacy is our highest principle.
